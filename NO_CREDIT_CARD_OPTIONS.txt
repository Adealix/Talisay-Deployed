   message.

   upgrade to paid plans or migrate to DigitalOcean.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes! Render and MongoDB Atlas are professional services used
   by thousands of companies. Enable 2FA for extra security.

Q: What if Render changes their free tier?
A: You can always migrate to another service. Your code works
   anywhere (it's just Node.js and Python).
